Why this page exists

Good friends. Permanently unavailable.

My current friend group is genuinely great. They are also impossible to pin down. Somewhere between work and everyone's separate lives, plans stopped being things we do and became things we discuss.

I got tired of the weekend that gets rescheduled five times until it quietly dies. So instead of waiting for that to fix itself, I am doing something slightly strange and putting this on the internet.

I am not trying to build a big circle. I want two or three people I can text on a Wednesday and actually see on Saturday.

Who I am hoping to meet

One filter that matters.

Roughly 24 to 30, any gender, anywhere in Ahmedabad. Into books, films, comedy, or building something of your own. Happier one on one than in a big group.

Honestly though, all of that is negotiable. The only thing that is not: you follow through. If you say Saturday, you mean Saturday. That is the whole filter.

How this works

Four steps, no app.

  1. You send a signal. The form below takes about two minutes.
  2. I read every single one. Personally, not a filter or a bot.
  3. If it clicks, I reach out on whichever contact you leave, and we make a real plan with a real date.
  4. We meet. Coffee, table tennis, a comedy night, a walk. Something actual, not a group chat that goes quiet in four days.
